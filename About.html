About Us

This website will guide you on the way to Svaneti

Welcome to Svaneti, a breathtaking region in the heart of the Caucasus Mountains, where ancient traditions meet stunning natural beauty. Svaneti, located in the northwestern part of Georgia, is a destination like no other, offering visitors a unique blend of adventure, history, and culture. Explore Svaneti.com we are trying to provide you with the information about Svaneti’s most interesting and remarkable places in the most convenient way, what will assist you to select the optimal routes and plan the tours effectively. Every initiative and person is welcome who wants to contribute in the popularization of Svaneti and development of its tourism potential. We believe that sharing the knowledge and experience with each other will make our journeys more comfortable and refreshing.
